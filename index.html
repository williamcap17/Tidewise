A practical roadmap

Future improvements

Tidewise is designed to begin useful with local demo data, then deepen its value through verified conditions, inclusive design, and trusted coastal partnerships.

01 · Live confidence

Verified conditions

Replace representative forecasts with source-labeled, real-time signals.

  • Weather, tide, UV, and water-quality feeds
  • Beach-operator status updates
  • Forecast confidence and last-updated times
02 · Trust & community

Thoughtful check-ins

Make crowd reports useful without turning the beach into a noisy social network.

  • Account-backed, rate-limited reports
  • Photo moderation and report expiry
  • Aggregated, privacy-safe presence counts
03 · Better access

Access for every traveler

Turn accessibility details into reliable trip planning information.

  • Step-free route and parking data
  • Beach wheelchair availability
  • Quiet, sensory, and service-animal guidance
04 · Responsible discovery

Coastal impact

Help visitors understand how to enjoy places without harming them.

  • Seasonal habitat closures
  • Leave-no-trace nudges at decision points
  • Local conservation donation pathways
05 · Partner value

Real partner portal

Move the dashboard from a preview to a secure, useful operating tool.

  • Consent-aware attribution and offers
  • Certification evidence workflows
  • Benchmarking and impact reporting
06 · Map depth

Native map layers

Graduate the illustrated map to an accessible mapping foundation.

  • Screen-reader map list equivalent
  • Transit, trail, restroom, and surf layers
  • Offline-first coastal trip packs

Suggested release sequence

Prioritize confidence and care before expanding social or commercial features.

NowUseful demoLocal saves, browseable beaches, crowd guidance, partner preview.
NextSource-connectedLive conditions and clear freshness indicators.
ThenTrusted communityModerated, privacy-respecting check-ins.
LaterPartner networkVerified certification and measurable local impact.

Every condition in this prototype is representative demo data. It is not a safety, weather, or lifeguard service.
